Zhenbin Yan

I am a Cryptographer at China Academy of Aerospace Electronics Technology. My research interests are in Cryptography, and its interplay with other areas in computer science, such as, Computational Complexity , Zero-Knowledge, Secure Multiparty Computation and Algorithm Design. I completed my PhD in 2019 at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences(UCAS) and State Key Laboratory of Information Security, Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China. Previously, I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Technology and Bachelors's degree in Mathematics from Xidian University, China..
